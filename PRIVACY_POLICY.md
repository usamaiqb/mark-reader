# Privacy Policy

**MarkReader**
**Last updated: June 22, 2026**

## Overview

MarkReader ("the App") is a local markdown and code file reader for Android. This privacy policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding that data.

## Data Collection and Usage

### Data that stays on your device

All file processing is done **locally on your device**. The App does not transmit any data to external servers. Specifically:

- **File contents** — Files you open or edit are read and displayed locally. No file content is uploaded or shared.

### Permissions

MarkReader requests **no permissions**. Files are opened through Android's standard content URI system, which does not require storage permission.

### Data the App does NOT collect

- The App does **not** collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.).
- The App does **not** use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
- The App does **not** transmit any data off your device.
- The App does **not** require or access an internet connection.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services that collect data.

## Data Retention

No data is stored beyond what you explicitly save to your device. Uninstalling the App removes all associated app data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The App does not collect personal information from any user.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

## Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's [GitHub repository](https://github.com/usamaiqb/mark-reader/issues).
